2D CAD Logo Design

This logo features a mobility vehicle designed for children with severe disabilities. I started by inputting the prompt "design a logo for a project focused on creating custom mobility vehicles for individuals with severe disabilities, specifically those with Coffin-Lowry Syndrome" into ChatGPT. The system generated a wheelchair symbol combined with a heart to symbolize mobility and care. After that, I asked it to refine the logo by adding playful elements, like bright colors and round shapes, to make it child-friendly. Once the initial draft was generated, I further customized it by enhancing the color scheme to evoke a sense of inclusivity and joy. This process ensured that the logos would be both visually appealing and easy to reproduce.

About the Project

This project aims to design custom mobility solutions for children with severe disabilities, particularly those with Coffin-Lowry Syndrome. By integrating playful and functional elements, we hope to create vehicles that provide both practical support and an engaging, enjoyable experience for children in need.
